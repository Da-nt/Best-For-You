The Steam Deck was created by Valve who was orginally a game developed who created classic games such as DOTA, half Life, Portal and many more. This time they've decided to use their experience and tackle the videogame console market.

The Valve Steam Deck

Think of this console as a portable gaming PC. Because that is exactly what it is trying to be. The Steam Deck specializes in bringin everything that a gaming PC can do to a portable platform.

Games


All Steam Deck Games

The Steam Deck brings games that aren't available on many consoles to a portable platform. So for the first time ever players can experience games like Portal 2, Half Life, CSGO and Team Fortress on the go.
